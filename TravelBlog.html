Top 3 Travel Destinations

1. Santorini, Greece

  • Whitewashed houses, blue-domed churches, and stunning sunsets
  • Best time to visit: April to October
  • Do not miss: Oia village sunset and Red Beach

2. Kyoto, Japan

  • Ancient temples, bamboo forests, and geisha traditions
  • Best time to visit: March to May or October to November
  • Do not miss: Arashiyama Bamboo Grove and Fushimi Inari Shrine

3. Banff National Park, Canada

  • Turquoise lakes, snow-capped mountains, and outdoor adventures
  • Best time to visit: June to August or December for snow activities
  • Do not miss: Lake Louise and the Icefields Parkway
